# StudyHub

It's an innovative platform that makes studying and sharing material easy and quick.

##  What is StudyHub ?

StudyHub is an application that connects people in terms of study resources, study notes, and updates in one place.

Imagine it's your **virtual study partner** 🤝

 User-friendly for both teacher and student
